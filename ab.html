Welcome to Our Website

We're working on a new attendance system for students and teachers. Students can easily mark their own attendance by using the application. Teachers can create a link for attendance, allowing students to mark their presence, check their attendance, and monitor their progress on a dashboard. Our system is user-friendly and eliminates the need for manual attendance, saving time for both students and teachers. This helps address the common issue of students struggling to maintain a 75% attendance requirement in schools and colleges. Among the many applications available, ours stands out by capturing the location of both the teacher creating the link and the student marking attendance. We aim to prevent proxy attendance by confirming if the student is physically present at the designated location.
